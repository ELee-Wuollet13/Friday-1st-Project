My first week at Epicodus was exciting!
We learned:

  • How to make a webpage
  • How to make it fancy with pictures and color
  • How to add block elements, collums improts from bootstrap (whcih has so far made me think of 'bootstrap bill' from Pirates of the Carribean every time)
  • And perhaps most importantly we have learned to save our work to GitHub so that no matter what happens on our computers, what we have made is safe and sound

    • The rapid changes in learning pace have been a bit jarring. On my first day I felt like I was picking things up slowly in git, but quicker in actual code. Second day I worked with two partners who knew what they were doing and we flew through the lesson third day I worked with two more advanced partners again. It was nice to see it done effectively, avoiding the first days frustrations was a welcome change but has now left me with a fair amount of review.
